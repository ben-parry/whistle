# About Whistle

Whistle is an opinionated punch clock for people who believe work has its hours and rest has its own.

## How It Works

You clock in when you start working, clock out when you stop. One session per day, just like a Victorian factory punch clock.

Work happens between 5am and 9pm, never on Sundays, and never more than 12 hours in a day. If you forget to clock out, Whistle clocks you out automatically.

## The Rules

- **Working hours**: 5:00 AM to 9:00 PM
- **Sundays**: No work allowed
- **Daily maximum**: 12 hours
- **Sessions**: One clock-in and one clock-out per day

## Your Year

The goal is 2,333 hours per year. Track your progress with the calendar heatmap and yearly total.

## The Leaderboard

See how you stack up against others. The leaderboard is public — no login required.

## Built With

Plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks. Hosted on Vercel with a Postgres database.
